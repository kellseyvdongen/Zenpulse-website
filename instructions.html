How to use your
ZenPulse Glove

Follow these simple steps to get started with your new ZenPulse Glove

Step 1: Unbox Your ZenPulse Glove

Carefully remove the ZenPulse Glove from its packaging. Ensure that all components, including the charging cable and user manual, are present.

Step 2: Charge the Glove

Connect the charging cable to the glove and plug it into a power source. Allow it to charge fully before first use, which may take up to 2 hours.

Step 3: Download the ZenPulse App

Visit the App Store or Google Play Store to download the ZenPulse app. Follow the on-screen instructions to install it on your smartphone.

Step 4: Carefully put on your glove

Slide your hand into the ZenPulse Glove, ensuring a snug fit. Adjust the straps if necessary to ensure comfort and optimal sensor contact with your skin.

Step 5: Pair the Glove with Your Smartphone

Open the ZenPulse app and follow the prompts to pair your glove via Bluetooth. Ensure that Bluetooth is enabled on your smartphone during this process.

Step 6: Customize Your Settings

Within the app, navigate to the settings menu to customize your preferences, such as notification alerts, heart rate zones, and data sync frequency.

Step 7: Start Using Your ZenPulse Glove

Once paired, you can start using your ZenPulse Glove to monitor your heart rate and receive real-time feedback through the app. Explore the app features to customize your experience.
